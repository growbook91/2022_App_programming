Yasuni in our prayers: Prayer Requests

1.Protection for Tagaeri and Taromenane groups

The indigenous groups living in the Yasuni National Park territory are in constant danger and do not deserve to live in such unstable environment. The oil companies threat does not only out their natural resources and agricultural goods in danger; it also puts these indegenous people's health and life span in danger. The side effects of oil liking on the soil and water has more consequences that we can imagine. We pray that God will protect these people and do justice to their cause and keep the most vulnerable groups in this community (children, pregnant women and the elderly) physically and mentally healthy.
Also, because of the lack of resources and limited contact with the outside world, treating diseases and secondary effects caused by oil pollution is especially hard. Prayer for medical help and regular check-ups is high;y needed, as well.

2. Discernment for the Ecuadorian government

The Ecuadorian government plays a big role in the future of these indegenous groups. Therefore, prayer for wisdom for the Ecuadorian authorities involved in this situation is needed. To pray for justice for the people who lost their land and cultural inheritance since that is their righteous propeerty and everything they have.
Also we pray that Ecuadorian authorities will not be driven by their economic interests and ambition but for a moral nad enviromental responsability. As fellow Ecuadorian, protecting our people's interests and rights should be everyone's goal. So, prayer for a united national spirit that seeks the well-being and protection of the Tagaeri and Taromenane people groups.

3. Healing for the nature

The enviromental impact that oil exploitation has on the Ecuadorian rain forest could be more serious that people imagine. Exotic species of plants and animals could disspaear forever in human intervention keeps spreading in the Yasuni territory. We ask for prayer to preserve God's wonderful creation in this land and all the precious creatures and plants that inhabit in there. All the toxic substances and chemicals contained in oil leakings have been causing mutations and malformations in endemic species. This meaning, several species are in danger of becoming extinct because of this issue.
May God purify the soil and move people to take action in the protection of Yasuni National Park for the future generations and the natural legacy to the world.

Sharon Vega
